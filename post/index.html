DocASAP Wrap-up

Thu, Aug 4, 2016 work

The day we arrived at DocASAP, we were assigned our first task: build the desks that we’d be using for the rest of the summer. Assembling IKEA furniture was not how I expected to start my summer internship at the health tech company, but it offered a surprisingly fun introduction to the startup lifestyle. Besides learning how to use a power drill (CEO Puneet Maheshwari was a great teacher), I have gained much from my experience at DocASAP.
