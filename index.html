Hi! I’m Daniel, a developer & designer.

I’m currently a student at Brown University concentrating in computer science. This past summer I worked as a software engineering intern at DocASAP, an online platform that enables patients to book appointments with the healthcare providers of their choice. In the past, I’ve worked with the team at HealthMap as a data science intern.

Besides health tech, I have a passion for education in computer science with a focus on inclusion for underrepresented minorities in the field. I work alongside other mentors on a team supported and funded by Google IgniteCS to run a coding club at a local high school in Providence.

You can view my full resume here

In my free time, I enjoy Olympic weightlifting, playing board games with my friends, and making simple 2D games.

Computer science is still magic to me; as I grow and develop more as a programmer I’m ultimately driven by the thrill of creating something fun and engaging out of nothing.
